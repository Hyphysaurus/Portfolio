### Featured projects (details)

#### Volta do Mar (Godot)

*Restore a fogbound archipelago through wind, wonder, and old-world secrets.*

**What it is**

An exploration game in progress about ecological restoration across an ocean archipelago. Blends **2D + 3D** gameplay with a mythic tone.

**Core pillars**

- Restoration gameplay with visible, lasting change
- Fog as navigation, progression, and mood
- Environmental storytelling through ruins, weather, and sound
- A strong sense of place: ocean, wind, distant landmarks

**Tech**

- Godot (2D + 3D)

**Current focus**

- Core loop prototype: fog clearing → restoration action → world change
- Traversal experiments (wind, sailing, gliding)
- One-island vertical slice with a clear before/after
