About

I'm Mario — an educator and creative with 7+ years in education and food service, now transitioning into software development and media production.

I've spent years leading teams, building curriculum, and working directly with people. That background shapes how I think about products: they should feel alive, invite exploration, and leave room for surprise.

I'm self-taught in development and trained in media through broadcasting and production coursework. When I'm not building, I'm reading history, learning languages, making music, taking photos, or getting lost in a good game.
